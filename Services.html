Our Services

At Kyle's PDR, we offer a range of paintless dent repair services to help restore your vehicle's appearance without the need for repainting.


Hail Damage Repair

Our technicians are skilled in repairing hail damage, which can cause multiple small dents across the surface of your vehicle. We use specialized tools to carefully remove these dents and restore your car's original shape.


Door Dings and Minor Dents


We also specialize in repairing door dings and other minor dents that may occur from everyday use. Our team ensures that your vehicle looks as good as new after our repair process.


Crease Repair

Creases can be particularly challenging to repair, but our experienced technicians have the expertise to handle these types of dents with precision and care, ensuring a seamless finish.
